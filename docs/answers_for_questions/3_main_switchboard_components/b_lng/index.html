Model B LNG description of MSB

Generator panel

  • SPACE HEATER, VCB OPEN, VCB CLOSE – indicators of the space heater and VCB state;
  • GEN RUN – indicator, which illuminates when the gen- erator is in operation;
  • POWER, POWER FACTOR – gauges for monitoring the generator parameters;
  • AMMETER – gauge with change-over five-position switch: OFF, R, S, T, OFF;
  • VOLTMETER – gauge with change-over five-position switch: BUS, R-S, S-T, T-R, OFF;
  • THERMO METER – digital indicators of the windings tem- peratures: “R” PHASE, “S” PHASE, “T” PHASE.
  • DE-EXCITATION – illuminated button with protection cover; click once to open the cover; click second time to de-excite the generator; the button is used when the generator is stopped, e.g. for maintenance; an alarm condition is activated if the button is used on running generator;
  • VCB ABNORMAL (RESET) – button; click to reset the VCB after an abnormal opening: the TRIP LED illuminates red on the multirelay;
  • READY TO START – indicator lamp; illuminates when the engine pre-start conditions are satisfied;
  • START ENGINE – illuminated button; click to start the gen- erator DE when the READY TO START lamp is illuminated;
  • STOP ENGINE – illuminated button; click to stop the gen- erator DE;
  • READY TO SYNCHRO – indicator lamp; illuminates when the generator is operating and ready to be synchro- nized with the bus;
  • SPACE HEATER – two-position operation switch to turn the space heater OFF/ON; when the heater is ON, blue indicator SPACE HEATER illuminates;
  • VOLTAGE CONTROL – two-position mode select switch:
  • MAN: operate the MVR regulator to adjust reactive power and voltage in parallel generators operation; Generator panel contains:
  • AUTO: adjust reactive power & voltage automatically;
  • MVR – regulator to adjust generator reactive power and voltage in MAN mode.

Main Generator VCB panel

  1. EARTHING SWITCH
    • placeholder for the switch handle; the handle ap- pears to be available when the door is open;
    • EARTHING OPERATION DOOR – button; click to open the door for operating the switch; the button is enabled when the breaker is in the DRAW OUT position;
    • OPEN, CLOSED – lamps; illuminate to indicate the EARTHING SWITCH position.
  2. BREAKER
    • handle switch; drag the handle to DRAW OUT position to start the maintenance procedure; the indicator lamp TEST illuminates;
    • SERVICE – lamp; illuminated when the breaker is ready for service (the handle is in INSERT position);
    • TEST – lamp; illuminated when the breaker is ready for maintenance (the handle is in DRAW OUT position);
  3. BREAKER
    • box, comprising the handle switch to trip the V.C.B. D1: drag the handle to EMCY TRIP position;
  4. Handle to unlock the front panel door

Measure and Synchro panel

  • BUS-TIE MODE – two-position select switch;

    • LOC : operate the HM1HM2 VCB from this panel;
    • REM : operate the ACB from the IAS displays POWER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM or GENERATOR CONTROL SYSTEM

  • BUS-TIE CB - two-position impulse OPEN /CLOSE switch to operate the bus tie VCB in LOC mode;

  • GEN # MODE - three-poistion selector switch:
  • REM AUTO : synchronization and VCB operation is performed by IAS;
  • AUTO LOC : synchronizing automatically, and the generator VCB is operated by the GEN # CB switch on this panel;
  • LOCAL MAN : synchronization and VCB operation is performed from this panel by the GEN # SPEED and GEN # CB switches.
  • GEN # CB – two-position impulse OPEN /CLOSE switch to operate the generator VCB.
  • GEN # CLOSED, GEN # OPEN – indicator lamps to show the generator VCB state.
